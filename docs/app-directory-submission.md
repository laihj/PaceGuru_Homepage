## Full Description

PaceGuru is the ultimate all-in-one running companion designed specifically for Apple Watch users. Whether you're training for your first 5K or chasing a marathon PR, PaceGuru helps you train smarter with data-driven insights.

### Key Features

**Seamless Apple Watch Sync**
Instantly import and analyze all your running data directly from your Apple Watch. No manual entry required.

**Advanced Training Analytics**
- Track your 30-day training load to prevent overtraining
- Monitor heart rate zones and pace distribution
- Visualize your running with hexagonal ability radar charts
- Analyze ground contact time, vertical oscillation, and more

**Scientific Training Plans**
- Create custom heart rate and pace zones
- Generate professional training plans (Hansons Marathon Method)
- Access classic workout templates (Yasso 800s, Fartleks, Intervals)
- Sync daily workouts directly to your Apple Watch

**Smart Running Tools**
- VDOT/VO₂max calculator with Jack Daniels' formulas
- Pace calculator for race predictions
- Training load monitoring
- Shoe mileage tracking

**AI-Powered Insights**
Get personalized feedback on every run to improve your form and performance.

### Why PaceGuru?

Most running apps just track your runs. PaceGuru helps you understand them. Built by runners, for runners, PaceGuru combines the latest exercise science (80/20 polarized training, VDOT systems) with practical tools to help you:

- Train at the right intensity (avoid the "moderate intensity trap")
- Monitor cumulative fatigue (30-day acute training load)
- Balance your training distribution
- Achieve breakthrough performances
